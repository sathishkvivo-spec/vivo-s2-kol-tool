S2 · Selection Logic

15 Parameters.

Framework for Tamil Nadu Gen Z. Brand-safe gate + Kevin SOP hard gates. No exceptions.

Premium profile & aesthetic

Does the KOL's visual identity reflect a polished, brand-worthy image?

Fits cafe culture

Does the KOL naturally create content in cafe/food/dining environments?

Weekend lifestyle creator

Does the KOL document outings, travel and social weekend experiences?

Young audience appeal

Does the KOL connect with Gen Z and young working professionals?

Natural storyteller

Does the KOL communicate authentically without scripted or forced delivery?

Strong photography & video quality

Does the KOL's content meet premium visual production standards?

Social & friend-oriented content

Does the KOL create group/social content, not just solo shoots?

Tamil Nadu relevance

Does the KOL have strong local cultural connect with TN audiences?

Lifestyle-first, not tech-first

Is the KOL's identity built around life experiences, not gadgets?

Good engagement

Does the KOL drive meaningful interaction relative to their reach?

Matches S2 personality

Does the KOL embody Young · Stylish · Curious · Explorer · Sharing?

MANDATORY GATE

Brand-safe profile

Is the KOL free from controversy, mature content or competitor conflicts?

Creates reusable content

Does the KOL produce content that can be repurposed across formats?

Comfortable with campaign guidelines

Does the KOL show experience working within brand briefs?

Long-term collaboration potential

Does the KOL show stability and longevity as a creator?
